Plugin for timedlinks.com

== Description ==

This plugin will allow you to activate timedlinks plugin on your site. In order
to use this plugin you must have a Timedlinks.com account. 

Go to timedlinks.com to create your account.

Your Timedlink Identifier or API Key is located in your SETUP.

If you want to set, change or manage the behavior of the pop-up on your site, 
you will need to do it from your Timedlinks account. 

The Pop-up won't be triggered until you've created one in your timedlinks account and 
set it to LIVE.

For more information please refer to the Timedlinks documentation.

== Installation ==

1. Install the Plugin or Upload 'Timedlinks for Wordpress' to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress
3. Provide your timedlinks identifier in the setting of the plugin
4. Enjoy !

== Widget.js Service ==

This script use your Timedlink Identifier to retrieve information from your timedlinks account and display pop-ups based on your configuration, strategies, triggers and design.

It injects an iframe into your wordpress site that allows us to communicate with our widget hadnler.

We update our service often for bug fixes, common issues, and upgrades.

The service do not share personal information from you of your website. It just allow us to retrieve
information from your Timedlink account in order to trigger and display your popup based on
your configuration.

You'll get notified in your Timedlinks Account whenever an update is made to our service.
